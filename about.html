About Mehari Redai Oil

From a vision in Tigray to one of Ethiopia’s leading edible oil producers

Message from the Founder

“My name is Ato Teklit Mehari. I started this factory with one dream: to produce the purest, healthiest cooking oil for every Ethiopian family while creating hundreds of jobs in Mekelle and Tigray.”

Today, with over 1 years of experience and a state-of-the-art refinery, we process more than 100 tons of sunflower and soybean seeds every day — all locally sourced when possible, fully refined, and delivered with pride.

— Ato Teklit Mehari
