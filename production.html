Production Process

From Seed to Pure Refined Oil – Full Control in Mekelle

1

Seed Reception & Cleaning

High-quality sunflower and soybean seeds arrive daily. We remove dust, stones, and impurities using modern cleaning machines to ensure only the best seeds enter production.

2

Mechanical Pressing

Seeds are pressed using high-capacity expellers to extract crude oil. The remaining cake is rich in protein and used as animal feed – zero waste philosophy.

3

Full RBD Refinery

Our state-of-the-art refinery performs three critical stages:

  • Degumming & Neutralization – removes gums and free fatty acids
  • Bleaching – activated clay removes color and impurities
  • Deodorization – steam distillation removes odor at 240°C under vacuum
4

Automated Bottling

Crystal-clear refined oil is automatically filled into PET bottles and jerrycans under strict hygienic conditions. Every bottle is sealed, labeled, and quality-checked before leaving the factory.
